How to Watch YouTube Productively.

In today's Internet Influenced world we watch a lot of YouTube videos (like Tutorails video and Productivity videos videos) and try to learn from them and try to grow. But wait do we learn and grow?

The clear answer to this question is NO because

  1. With time we forget what we learned. (The forgetting Curve)
  2. We don't apply to our lives what we have learned.
  3. We binge-watch Tutorails video and Productivity videos videos as other entertainment videos.

Even why should we care

Aristotle said: â€œWe are what we repeatedly do". But today, we are what we repeatedly consume. That's why we should be very precise about what we consume. Now by combining those two quotes, it is concluded that We should be very precise about what we are consuming on the internet simultaneously we must practically apply those learned things to life otherwise unknowingly we'll make a habit of consuming content on the internet without learning from it.

Steps to Watch YouTube productively

It is an easy 3 step process

Step-I Choosing the Right Video

Find the right video for you, It may vary from person to person but one thing that will be common in all videos is it will help you to achieve what you want to achieve and it will not waste your time.

Step-II watching the video the correctly

The second step is to carefully watch the video with intense focus and understand the entire video. Now play that video again and start taking notes on the notion or in your YouTube Diary. Keep in mind that notes should be point by point and short, So we can easily read and understand them in the future.

Step-III Beating the Forgetting Curve

Now you have to revise the notes every day at bedtime before falling asleep and think about those scenarios where you can apply those things in your life. This is the one most important single habit that can transform your life. So my suggestion is must do it every night.

Conclusion

Try doing this daily only for one week and see results/changes in yourself. It helped me a lot and I guarantee it will help you.
