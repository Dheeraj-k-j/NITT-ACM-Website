VIRTUAL HAWKER

Description:You will need to build a website by completing all the subtasks. In total there will be 3 levels. Level 0, Level 1 and bonus level.

Rules:

  • At the beginning of the game, each team will be having 100 points.1st team to guess the picture correctly will gain 50 points, 2nd 49 and so on. Basically, you will get 1 point lesser than the previous team who guesses the image correctly. After 35 correct guesses for the image, further teams will get 15 points each for guessing the image correctly.
  • Hints & Lifelines:
    • If your team gets stuck than you can take the help of hints/lifelines, but that would cost you some points-
    • 1st hint:- 3 points
    • 2nd hint:- 5 points
    • First letter lifeline:- 10 points
    • Total no of letters lifeline:- 10 points
  • Use only lowercase alphabets/digits, for eg. Type "A.P.J Abdul Kalam 1931" as "apjabdulkalam1931" (without quotes)
  • Any attempt of attack on the site is intolerable. The player will be blacklisted and will not be able to participate in any other events.
