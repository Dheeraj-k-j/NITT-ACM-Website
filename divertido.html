DIVERTIDO

Description: It is a fun event, in which players will be given some short videos and they will have to use their creativity to caption those videos to make them funny.

Rules:

  • Submissions will be taken on the Instagram page of ACM.
  • Max three submissions will be allowed per player.
  • No submission from the player should target any of the faculties or seniors.
  • Winners will be decided on basis of engagement on their post(s) and how relatable they are. The more fun it is, the better it will be.
