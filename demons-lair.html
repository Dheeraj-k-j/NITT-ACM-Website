Demon's Lair

Description:“DEMON’S LAIR” is a PC game in which you have to kill zombies to score. In this game, you are trapped in the demon’s lair. You have to find the boss (who has some special powers including teleportation) and kill him to escape from the lair and move to the next place where you have to kill more and more zombies to survive. There will be a surprise in the new place. You have to keep your position on the leaderboard to win the prize.

Rules:

  • Make sure you are connected to the internet while playing the game otherwise, your score will not be updated on the leaderboard. It is your responsibility to connect your system with the internet.
  • Connect an external mouse to your system for aiming and firing.
  • Use valid information while registering on the website otherwise You will not be eligible for prizes.
  • Use (Arrow keys or W/S/A/D ) + LEFT SHIFT for running. Click on the INFO button on the home page of the game to see other controls.
